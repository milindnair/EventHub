Modify your Event

Welcome back! We're happy to help you make changes to your event. Please fill out the form below with the updated information.

Please follow the steps mentioned below on how to modify your event.

  • Fill out the form below with the updated event details.

  • Select the total number of rooms/halls/campus area that you require for the event.

  • Then click on room layout to view the rooms and modify your room bookings.

  • After submitting the updated form, a request will be sent to the HOD for approval.

  • Once your modifications are approved, they will be reflected in the event calendar.
